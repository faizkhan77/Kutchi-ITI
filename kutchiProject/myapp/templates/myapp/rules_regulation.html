RULES AND REGULATIONS

Admission

  • Students have to bring ID Card and attendance chart daily.
  • At the time of Admission, students must bring any one of the following: Xerox copies of Education & ID proof, Marksheet / leaving certificate & Aadhaar Card / Passport / Election Card.
  • Passport size photos required:
    • 2 photos at the time of Admission,
    • 2 photos at the time of Exam,
    • 1 photo for Certificate.
  • Please maintain discipline as you are being watched by CCTV.
  • If a student remains absent, he/she is liable to pay a fine of Rs 5/- per day.
  • Parents can meet the Management for any inquiries/queries on Wednesday and Saturday between 11:00 am to 12:30 pm.
  • If a student remains absent continuously for 15 days, admission will be canceled.
  • Students will have to pay for damages if any to the institute's property or instruments are damaged by him/her.
  • Misbehavior of students will cause cancellation of admission.
  • Every student who is registering / taking admission in our institute will be on his/her own responsibility.
  • No illegal or criminal activities will be performed or be a part of them. If found, strict action will be taken on him/her and admission will be canceled.
  • It is Parent's/Student's responsibility to inform the institute of any change in Address or Contact numbers.
  • Students have to take care of their own belongings; the institute will not be responsible for any loss.
  • If a student is Physically/Medically unfit, Student/Parents must inform the institute at the time of admission, and parents will be responsible for any untoward incidents.
  • If any Institute-related photos or comments are posted on Social Media, Strict Legal Action will be taken against the student.
  • For any query/Complaint regarding students, use our Complaint Box or call admin department.
  • Use of Any Social Media Site in the institute is prohibited.
  • Keep the classroom clean and neat at all times.
  • Consumption of food and sweet drinks are strictly not allowed in the institute.
  • Read the institute notice board regularly.
  • Use of all forms of electronic gadgets is prohibited unless they are required for course/lesson. Eg. Electronic dictionaries etc.
  • Boys are not allowed to wear earrings/ear studs, ear sticks, or have any other forms of body piercing and should always come in Formal Dress.
  • The institute reserves the right to confiscate any items that are prohibited, and Legal action will be taken against the student.
  • No Vandalism. Don't write or carve on your desk or institute property.
  • Library facility available.
  • Treat computers with care.
  • Smoking and the use of alcohol, gutka, pan-masala, or other drugs are strictly Prohibited. Strict action will be taken against students.
  • Every student should show respect and use appropriate language in communicating with teachers and other students.
  • Any gifts and personal works between students / staffs not allowed.
  • Every student must obey and respect their heads, teachers, and staff of the institute.
  • Student should come and leave institute on time.
  • Do your assignments on time.
  • Railway concession pass facility available for students living outside Mumbra.
  • Letter to Trust/Organization for scholarship available on request.
  • Institute is always ready to help students for Future technical support.
  • 100% job Assistance. To crack the interview is the student's responsibility.

Attendance

  • 80% attendance compulsory in order to appear for final exam for board / institute.
  • Attendance by Finger punch on Biometric machine and Attendant sheet is Compulsory.
  • Signature of teachers on the attendant sheet is compulsory, and parents should check the attendant sheet daily.
  • Application is compulsory for leave with Genuine reason.
  • Institute will provide leave for a maximum of 3 months; else, admission will be canceled.

Fees

  • Mode of payment: CASH / CHEQUE / DD / CREDIT CARD / DEBIT CARD
  • Fees should be paid before commencement of the course.
  • After paying fees, please collect fees receipt.
  • Exam fees Extra depends on Board and Course
  • Govt. Service Taxes will be charged extra.
  • Fees once paid are not refundable or transferable under any circumstances.
  • EMI should be paid before the 10th of every month; otherwise, a late fee of Rs 100/- will be charged.
  • If a cheque is bounced / returned, a fine of Rs 500/- will be charged.
  • Please draw Cheque / DD in favor of: Kutchi Indian Technical Institute.

Course

  • Our courses are endorsed as per industries specifications.
  • Mumbra's Pioneer Institute with 27 yrs of Quality training & Genuine Certification with reasonable fees for your Bright Future.
  • Students have to complete their course within the given time period or else he / she will have to pay Rs 650/- to Rs. 1000/- extra per month till the course is completed.
  • Course material is available with a reasonable price.
  • Fast Track courses available.
  • Special weekend batches for working people available.
  • If the course is upgraded, the student will have to pay extra service tax and remaining fees of the upgraded course.
  • Flexible batch is available only on the condition that a teacher or seat is available.

Exam

  • Fill proper exam form and pay exam fees.
  • 2 passport size photographs required.
  • It is mandatory for students to be present 15 minutes before at the Examination center.
  • Students must bring their examination Hall Ticket in the exam hall.
  • Students will not be allowed to appear for the exam without Hall Ticket.
  • Latest passport size photograph showing clearly the face should be fixed at the space provided on the exam form. A photograph with sun glasses is not acceptable.
  • If a student remains absent in the exam, he/she will be assumed failed.
  • For 2nd or 3rd attempt examination, exam fees plus extra Rs 300/- will be charged.
  • Rs 300/- will be charged for exam paper/answer sheet revaluation.
  • If a student is caught cheating in the exam, he/she will not be allowed to continue the paper and considered fail.
  • If a student fails in the exam, he/she can sit for re-exam, 2nd and 3rd attempt, if failed in the 3rd attempt, he/she has to complete Re-Admission procedure.
  • Results will be declared within 1 month from the exam date.
  • Result will be notified by SMS.
  • Vocational courses exams facilities available for Outside students.

Certificate

  • Students have to bring the hall ticket and 1 passport size photo at the time of collecting the certificate.
  • Certificate and marksheet will be issued a minimum of 1 month from the date of examination.
  • Students have to sign on the certificate.
  • Genuineness of Certificate can be verified online by any individual / company in India/Abroad for a job.
  • An affidavit is required for a duplicate certificate.
  • If the certificate is not collected within one year, then a penalty of Rs 300/- will be charged per year.
Rules & Regulations Are Made By Institute.
Please read and understood the above rules and regulations and agree to abide by it.
